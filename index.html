📢 Caleb University Announces Admission for 2025/2026 Academic Session

Read More ▼

Caleb University, Imota, Lagos State, has officially opened admissions for the 2025/2026 academic session into its distinguished programmes in Criminology & Security Studies and the newly introduced Peace & Conflict Studies (PCS). Criminology & Security Studies is an interdisciplinary programme that explores the causes, consequences, and prevention of crime while equipping students with the knowledge and strategies required to maintain safety and security in society. Peace & Conflict Studies, now available as a new course, is designed to prepare students for leadership roles in dispute resolution, peacebuilding, and human rights advocacy. The programme provides a comprehensive understanding of the factors that drive conflicts — political, economic, cultural, social, and environmental — and offers strategies for effective conflict management and de-escalation. Graduates of this programme will be well-positioned to work with the United Nations, NGOs, and government agencies worldwide.

✅ Admission Requirements

Prospective students are required to have credit passes in:

  • English Language
  • Mathematics
  • Government or History
  • Economics
  • Any other subject

Applications are open from August 1 to November 31, 2025.
